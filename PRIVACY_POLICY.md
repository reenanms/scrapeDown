# Privacy Policy for ScrapeDown

ScrapeDown stores scraped content and user configuration locally in your browser. No data is transmitted to external servers by the extension. If you use the extension to scrape content, it remains on your device unless you manually copy or share it.

We do not collect, transmit, or sell any personal information. For questions or concerns, contact the developer via the Chrome Web Store listing.
